Hung Nguyen is a lecturer at the Faculty of Information Technology, [Nha Trang University, Vietnam](http://ntu.edu.vn). He received his PhD in IT from [Deakin University Australia](https://www.deakin.edu.au) in 2020. Previously, he received MSc in ICE ([Yeungnam University](http://www.yu.ac.kr), the Republic of Korea, 2009), Engineer's Degree in IT ([Hanoi University of Technology](https://hust.edu.vn/), Vietnam, 2001). 

## Research topics
- Applied machine learning
- Data science, social media analytics
- Graph neural networks

## Teaching courses 
- Introduction to Computer Programming (with C)
- Programming Techniques (with C++)
- Object-oriented Programming (with C#)
- [Big Data Technology](https://nd-hung.github.io/Big-Data/)
